這篇由愛因斯坦（A. Einstein）於 1916 年發表的論文《廣義相對論的基礎》（The Foundation of the General Theory of Relativity）包含了以下完整的章節標題：

**§ 3. 時空連續體；表達自然界普遍定律的方程式須滿足一般協變性要求 (The Space-Time Continuum. Requirement of General Co-Variance for the Equations Expressing General Laws of Nature)** 
**§ 4. 四個坐標與空間、時間測量之間的關係 (The Relation of the Four Co-ordinates to Measurement in Space and Time)** 

### B. 關於廣義協變方程式的數學辅助方法 (Mathematical Aids to the Formulation of Generally Covariant Equations)

* **§ 13. 真空中的重力場方程式 (The Field Equations of Gravitation in Vacuum)**
* **§ 14. 哈密頓原則與重力場方程 (The Hamilton Principle and the Field Equations of Gravitation)**
* **§ 15. 能量與動量守恆 (Conservation of Energy and Momentum)**

* **§ 20. 重力紅移 (The Gravitational Redshift of Spectral Lines)**

8. 物理學中的時間觀念 (On the Idea of Time in Physics)
9. 同時性的相對性 (The Relativity of Simultaneity)
10. 距離觀念的相對性 (On the Relativity of the Conception of Distance)
11. 勞侖茲變換 (The Lorentz Transformation)
12. 運動中的量尺與時鐘之行為 (The Behaviour of Measuring-Rods and Clocks in Motion)
13. 速度相加定理：斐索實驗 (Theorem of the Addition of Velocities. The Experiment of Fizeau)

21. 古典力學的基礎與狹義相對論在哪些方面不能令人滿意？ (In what Respects are the Foundations of Classical Mechanics and of the Special Theory of Relativity Unsatisfactory?)

28. 廣義相對性原理的一般表述 (Exact Formulation of the General Principle of Relativity)
